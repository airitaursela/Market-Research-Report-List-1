<p><p>The Aramid Fiber Reinforced Composite market is categorized into thermoplastic and thermoset types. Thermoplastic composites feature polymers that can be repeatedly reformed when heated, offering advantages like recyclability and ease of processing. They are widely used in automotive and aerospace applications. In contrast, thermoset composites are formed through a curing process, resulting in a rigid and heat-resistant structure. These are typically utilized in applications requiring high strength and temperature stability, such as military and industrial sectors.</p></p>
