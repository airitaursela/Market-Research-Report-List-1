<p><p>The hot-pressed magnets market primarily consists of injection magnets and pressed magnets. Injection magnets are produced using a process that combines magnetic powders with thermoplastics, allowing for intricate shapes and high precision. These are ideal for applications requiring complex geometries. Pressed magnets, on the other hand, are manufactured by compacting magnetic powders under high pressure and temperature, resulting in high-density materials. Both types cater to various industries, including automotive, electronics, and energy, offering tailored magnetic solutions for specific requirements.</p></p>
