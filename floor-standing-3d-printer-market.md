<p><strong>The Floor-standing 3D Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FDM（FusedDepositionModel）</li><li>SLA（Stereolithography）</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The floor-standing 3D printer market includes two primary types: FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling) and SLA (Stereolithography). FDM printers work by extruding thermoplastic filaments to build objects layer by layer, offering durability and cost-effectiveness, ideal for prototyping and functional parts. In contrast, SLA printers use a laser to cure liquid resin into solid layers, providing high-resolution prints with smooth finishes, suitable for intricate designs and detailed models. Each type caters to specific applications and user needs in various industries.</p></p>
