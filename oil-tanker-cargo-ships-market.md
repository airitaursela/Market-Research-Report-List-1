<p><strong>The Oil Tanker Cargo Ships Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>VLCC</li><li>ULCC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil tanker cargo ship market primarily consists of Very Large Crude Carriers (VLCCs) and Ultra Large Crude Carriers (ULCCs). VLCCs typically carry between 200,000 to 320,000 deadweight tons (DWT) of crude oil, making them ideal for long-haul transportation. ULCCs, larger than VLCCs, can transport over 320,000 DWT, enabling greater economies of scale. Both types are crucial for international oil trade, affecting shipping rates, supply chain logistics, and the global oil market dynamics.</p></p>
