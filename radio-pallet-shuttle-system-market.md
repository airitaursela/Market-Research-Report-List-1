<p><ul><li>LIFO Type</li><li>FIFO Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Radio Pallet Shuttle System market consists of two primary types: LIFO (Last In, First Out) and FIFO (First In, First Out). LIFO systems prioritize the retrieval of the most recently stored pallets, making them suitable for industries with short product life cycles. In contrast, FIFO systems ensure that older stock is accessed first, promoting inventory rotation and minimizing spoilage, which is particularly beneficial for perishable goods. Both systems enhance storage efficiency and optimize warehouse operations based on specific inventory management needs.</p></p>
