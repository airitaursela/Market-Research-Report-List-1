<p><p>Phenolic resin plays a crucial role in the electronic products market, primarily categorized into thermoplastic and thermosetting types. Thermoplastic phenolic resin offers flexibility and easy processing, making it suitable for applications requiring reshaping under heat. In contrast, thermosetting phenolic resin is characterized by its high thermal stability and durability, making it ideal for structural components in electronic devices. Both types are valued for their excellent electrical insulation properties, contributing to the performance and reliability of electronic products.</p></p>
