<p><p>The feed enzymes market includes various types such as pectinase, xylanase, cellulose, mannase, and glucanase, each playing a crucial role in animal nutrition. Pectinase aids in breaking down pectin, enhancing nutrient absorption, while xylanase targets hemicellulose, improving fiber digestion. Cellulose assists in digesting fibrous materials, boosting energy availability. Mannase enhances the digestion of mannans in feed, and glucanase breaks down beta-glucans, facilitating better nutrient utilization. Together, these enzymes optimize feed efficiency and overall animal health.</p></p>
