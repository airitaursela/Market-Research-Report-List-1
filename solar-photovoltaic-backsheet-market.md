<p><strong>The Solar Photovoltaic Backsheet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>KPK Structure</li><li>KPE Structure</li><li>TPE Structure</li><li>PPE Structure</li><li>Other Types</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar photovoltaic backsheet market includes several types based on material composition and structural properties. KPK structure consists of a polymer layer sandwiched between two protective layers, offering durability. KPE structure uses polyethylene as a primary material, known for its UV resistance. TPE structure incorporates thermoplastic elastomers, providing flexibility and durability. PPE structure, made from polyphenylene ether, emphasizes thermal stability and electrical insulation. Other types may include hybrid materials or specialized structures to meet specific performance requirements in various solar applications.</p></p>
