<p><ul><li>Active Towed Array Sonar</li><li>Passive Towed Array Sonar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The towed array sonar market is divided into two main types: active and passive systems. Active towed array sonar transmits sonar signals and analyzes returning echoes to detect and classify underwater objects, offering precise targeting capabilities. In contrast, passive towed array sonar relies on receiving sounds generated by other vessels or marine life, enabling stealthy surveillance without emitting signals. Both types play critical roles in naval operations, underwater research, and maritime security by enhancing situational awareness and tracking capabilities.</p></p>
