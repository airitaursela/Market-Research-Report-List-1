<p><p>The Composites Consumables Market encompasses various products essential for composite manufacturing, including films, peel plies, flow nettings, and surfacing veils. Films serve as barriers or release agents during the curing process, enhancing surface quality. Peel plies facilitate easy removal of excess resin and enhance bonding. Flow nettings improve resin distribution during infusion processes, ensuring uniformity. Surfacing veils provide an excellent surface finish and protect against moisture absorption, contributing to the overall durability and performance of composite structures.</p></p>
