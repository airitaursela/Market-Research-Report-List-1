<p><p>The neonatal warming equipment market comprises two primary types: radiation heating and electrical heating. Radiation heating systems, such as radiant warmers, provide controlled warmth from above, ensuring optimal thermal regulation for vulnerable newborns. In contrast, electrical heating devices, like incubators, maintain a stable environment by using heated air or surfaces, protecting infants from hypothermia while allowing for monitoring and care. Both methods play critical roles in neonatal care, enhancing survival rates and promoting healthy development in premature or low-birth-weight infants.</p></p>
