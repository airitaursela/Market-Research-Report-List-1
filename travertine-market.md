<p><strong>The Travertine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnesian Travertine</li><li>High-Calcium Travertine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The travertine market is divided mainly into two types: Magnesian Travertine and High-Calcium Travertine. Magnesian Travertine contains higher magnesium content, making it suitable for various architectural and decorative applications, often favored for its unique coloration and durability. In contrast, High-Calcium Travertine, which has a predominance of calcium carbonate, is primarily used in flooring, wall cladding, and garden features due to its lighter color and ease of carving. Both types cater to distinct preferences in construction and design.</p></p>
