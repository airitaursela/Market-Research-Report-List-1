<p><p>The two-wheeler in-wheel motor market comprises two main types: outer rotor and inner rotor motors. Outer rotor motors feature the rotor on the outside, providing a larger surface area for better torque and cooling, enhancing efficiency in urban commuting. In contrast, inner rotor motors have the rotor positioned inside the stator, resulting in compact designs that offer better control and responsiveness. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, impacting the overall performance and design of electric two-wheelers.</p></p>
