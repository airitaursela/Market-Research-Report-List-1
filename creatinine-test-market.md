<p><p>The creatinine test market comprises three primary types: Blood Tests, Urine Tests, and Creatinine Clearance Tests. Blood Tests measure the level of creatinine in the bloodstream, indicating kidney function. Urine Tests assess creatinine levels in urine over a specific period, helping to evaluate renal health. Creatinine Clearance Tests combine blood and urine measurements to estimate the kidney's filtration capacity, providing a comprehensive assessment of renal function. Each type plays a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring kidney-related conditions.</p></p>
