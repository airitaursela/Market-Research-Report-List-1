<p><strong>The Polybutene Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PB-1</li><li>PIB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polybutene market comprises primarily two types: PB-1 and PIB. PB-1, known as polybutylene, is a thermoplastic polymer used in applications requiring durability, flexibility, and chemical resistance, such as piping and automotive components. PIB, or polyisobutylene, serves as a rubber-like synthetic polymer, valued for its adhesive properties and gas barrier capabilities, making it ideal for sealants, coatings, and adhesives. Both types cater to diverse industries, including construction, automotive, and consumer goods, driving their demand in the market.</p></p>
