<p><p>The digital imaging system market encompasses various modalities, including X-ray, fluoroscopy, ultrasound, and magnetic resonance imaging (MRI). X-ray systems provide static images for diagnostics, while fluoroscopy offers real-time imaging to observe dynamic processes. Ultrasound uses high-frequency sound waves for non-invasive examinations, particularly in obstetrics and cardiology. MRI employs strong magnetic fields and radio waves to produce detailed images of soft tissues, offering insights into complex conditions. Together, these technologies enhance diagnostic capabilities in healthcare.</p></p>
