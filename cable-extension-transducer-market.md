<p><p>A Cable-Extension Transducer (CET) is an electromechanical device that converts linear displacement into an electrical signal. It typically consists of a cable attached to a sensing mechanism, allowing it to measure movement and position over extended distances. CETs are widely used in various applications, including construction, automotive testing, and robotics, owing to their high accuracy, durability, and resistance to environmental factors.</p><p>The Cable-Extension Transducer Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.7% during the forecast period.
