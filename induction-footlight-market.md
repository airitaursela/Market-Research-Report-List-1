<p><p>The induction footlight market encompasses various types of sensor technologies, primarily infrared sensor lights and radar sensor lights. Infrared sensor lights detect motion through heat emitted by objects, making them ideal for energy-efficient outdoor and indoor lighting. Radar sensor lights utilize microwave signals to detect movement, offering a broader detection range and enhanced reliability in diverse conditions. Additionally, other types of induction footlights may include ultrasonic and photoelectric sensors, catering to specific needs and preferences in lighting solutions.</p></p>
