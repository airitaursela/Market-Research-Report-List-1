<p><strong>The DNA and Gene Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oligonucleotide DNA microarrays (oDNA)</li><li>Complementary DNA microarrays (cDNA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DNA and gene chip market comprises primarily two types: oligonucleotide DNA microarrays (oDNA) and complementary DNA microarrays (cDNA). Oligonucleotide microarrays utilize short, synthetic DNA fragments to detect specific sequences, enabling precise gene expression analysis and SNP detection. In contrast, cDNA microarrays use cloned DNA fragments derived from reverse-transcribed mRNA, allowing researchers to explore gene expression profiles on a broader scale. Both technologies play critical roles in genomics, diagnostics, and personalized medicine, facilitating advancements in molecular biology and therapeutic development.</p></p>
