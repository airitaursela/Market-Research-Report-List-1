<p><ul><li>Ceiling Mounted</li><li>Base Stands</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The trapeze devices market is divided into two main types: ceiling-mounted and base stands. Ceiling-mounted trapeze devices are secured to overhead structures, providing stability and allowing users to maneuver with ease during rehabilitation or daily activities. In contrast, base stands are freestanding units that offer portability and versatility, enabling users to reposition them as needed. Both types serve to enhance mobility and support for individuals with limited mobility, ensuring safety and comfort during use.</p></p>
