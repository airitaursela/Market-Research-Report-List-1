This model offers convenience and a predictable system for locations and availability.</p><p>3. **Free-Floating**: Cars can be picked up and dropped off anywhere within a designated area, providing flexibility and spontaneity for users. Each type caters to different user preferences and urban mobility needs.</p></p>
