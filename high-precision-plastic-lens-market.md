<p><p>The High Precision Plastic Lens market is primarily divided into two types: aspheric lenses and spherical lenses. Aspheric lenses feature a non-spherical surface profile, allowing for improved image quality and reduced optical aberrations, making them ideal for high-performance applications. In contrast, spherical lenses maintain a consistent curvature, suited for basic optical functions and cost-effective solutions. Both types serve diverse industries, including consumer electronics, automotive, and medical devices, driving advancements in optical technology and enhancing product performance.</p></p>
