<p><strong>The Intraoral Phosphor Screen Scanners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pointer Type</li><li>Digital Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Intraoral phosphor screen scanners are dental imaging devices that utilize phosphor plates to capture and convert X-ray images into digital format. The market features two primary types: Pointer Type and Digital Type. Pointer Type scanners require manual positioning of the phosphor plate within the patient's mouth, offering a cost-effective solution for practices with lower imaging needs. In contrast, Digital Type scanners automate the process, providing enhanced image quality, faster workflow, and integration with digital dental systems, catering to advanced dental practices.</p></p>
