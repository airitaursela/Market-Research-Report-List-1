<p><strong>The Reaction Bonded Silicon Carbide (RBSiC) Ceramics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid Silicon Infiltration (LSI)</li><li>Gaseous Silicon Infiltration (GSI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Reaction Bonded Silicon Carbide (RBSiC) ceramics market primarily features two types of manufacturing processes: Liquid Silicon Infiltration (LSI) and Gaseous Silicon Infiltration (GSI). LSI involves infiltrating a pre-formed silicon carbide structure with liquid silicon, enhancing density and mechanical properties. In contrast, GSI uses vapor-phase silicon to penetrate a porous silicon carbide framework, resulting in lighter and finer microstructures. Both methods cater to diverse applications, including aerospace, automotive, and electronics, driven by their superior thermal and mechanical characteristics.</p></p>
