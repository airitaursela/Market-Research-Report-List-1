<p><p>The naval ship propeller market is categorized into three main types: Fixed Pitch Propellers (FPP), Controllable Pitch Propellers (CPP), and Others. Fixed Pitch Propellers are designed for efficiency at a specific speed and are widely used in vessels with steady operational profiles. Controllable Pitch Propellers allow for real-time adjustments to the blade angle, enhancing maneuverability and efficiency across various speeds. The "Others" category includes specialized or hybrid propeller types that cater to unique operational requirements or advanced technologies in naval applications.</p></p>
