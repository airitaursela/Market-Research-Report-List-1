<p><p>Filtering tanks are categorized into Manual and Fully Automatic markets based on their operational mode. In contrast, Fully Automatic filtering tanks utilize advanced technology for automated operations, including real-time monitoring and self-cleaning features. This efficiency reduces labor costs and minimizes human error, making them ideal for large-scale operations that require consistent performance and minimal downtime.</p></p>
