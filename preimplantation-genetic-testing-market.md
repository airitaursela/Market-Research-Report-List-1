<p><ul><li>Preimplantation Genetic Screening</li><li>Preimplantation Genetic Diagnosis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Preimplantation Genetic Testing market comprises two primary types: Preimplantation Genetic Screening (PGS) and Preimplantation Genetic Diagnosis (PGD). PGS is utilized to identify chromosomal abnormalities in embryos, ensuring only genetically viable embryos are selected for implantation, thereby enhancing the chances of a successful pregnancy. In contrast, PGD focuses on detecting specific genetic disorders in embryos for couples at risk of passing on hereditary conditions. Together, these testing methods support informed reproductive decisions and improve overall outcomes in assisted reproductive technologies.</p></p>
