<p><ul><li>Buckle Folders</li><li>Combi Folders</li><li>Knife Folders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paper folder market comprises several types, including buckle folders, combi folders, and knife folders. Buckle folders utilize a set of buckle rollers to fold sheets accurately, making them suitable for lighter paper stocks. Combi folders combine features of both buckle and knife technologies, providing versatility for various paper types and thicknesses. Knife folders, on the other hand, employ a sharp blade to achieve precise folds, ideal for thicker materials and high-speed production. Each type caters to different folding needs in the printing and packaging industries.</p></p>
