Thru-Hull Mount lights are installed by penetrating the hull, providing a sleek and integrated design, ideal for deep-water vessels. Surface Mount lights attach to the hull's exterior, offering easier installation and maintenance, suitable for smaller boats. The "Others" category includes innovative lighting solutions like remote-controlled and LED options, catering to diverse marine needs and preferences for aesthetics and functionality.</p></p>
