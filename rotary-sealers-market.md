<p><p>The rotary sealers market is categorized into horizontal and vertical rotary sealers. Horizontal rotary sealers operate with a horizontal motion, allowing for efficient sealing of pouches or containers that pass through the machine, ideal for products requiring a flat sealing surface. In contrast, vertical rotary sealers use a vertical orientation, suited for sealing applications involving tall or bulky items, providing flexibility in packaging designs. Both types enhance production efficiency and are crucial in industries like food, pharmaceuticals, and packaging.</p></p>
