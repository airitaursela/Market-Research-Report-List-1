<p><strong>The Desoldering Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plunger type</li><li>Bulb type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The desoldering pumps market features two primary types: plunger type and bulb type. Plunger type pumps utilize a spring-loaded mechanism that, when pressed, creates a suction effect to remove molten solder. They are known for their precision and ease of use. In contrast, bulb type pumps operate with a flexible bulb that, when squeezed, generates suction to draw away solder. They are simple and effective but may require more user experience for efficient operation. Both types cater to different user preferences in electronics repair.</p></p>
