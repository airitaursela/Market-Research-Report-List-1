<p><p>The Surface-To-Air Missiles (SAM) market can be categorized into three main types: high-altitude missiles, hollow missiles, and low-altitude missiles. High-altitude missiles are designed to intercept targets at considerable heights, effectively countering aerial threats from great distances. Low-altitude missiles focus on engaging targets flying at lower altitudes, making them ideal for short-range defense. Hollow missiles combine features of both, offering versatility in targeting across various altitudinal ranges. These classifications enhance strategic defense capabilities in different operational environments.</p></p>
