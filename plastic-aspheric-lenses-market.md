<p><p>The Plastic Aspheric Lenses market encompasses various manufacturing techniques, each serving specific application needs. Compression molding involves shaping plastic materials under heat and pressure to create precise lens forms, making it suitable for high-volume production with consistent quality. Injection molding, on the other hand, injects molten plastic into molds, offering intricate designs and faster production cycles. Lastly, turning molding involves rotating molds to form lens shapes, often employed for larger or more complex designs, ensuring versatility in lens applications across industries.</p></p>
