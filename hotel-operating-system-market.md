<p><p>The hotel operating system market can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise systems. Cloud-based solutions allow hotels to access their operating systems via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and reduced maintenance costs. They support remote operations and real-time updates. In contrast, on-premise systems are installed directly on a hotel's local servers, providing more control and data security but requiring higher upfront costs and ongoing IT management. Both types serve unique operational needs within the hospitality industry.</p></p>
