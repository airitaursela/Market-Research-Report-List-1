<p><p>The Hybrid Fiber-Coaxial (HFC) cable market features two primary types of fiber: single-mode and multimode. Single-mode fiber is designed for long-distance communication, offering higher bandwidth and faster data transmission by allowing light to travel straight down the fiber. Multimode fiber, on the other hand, supports shorter distances, using multiple light modes to transmit data. Both types cater to various applications in telecommunications, enhancing data delivery and connectivity in HFC networks, while addressing specific needs based on distance and throughput.</p></p>
