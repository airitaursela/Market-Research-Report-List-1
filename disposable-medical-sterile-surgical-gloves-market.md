<p><p>The Disposable Medical Sterile Surgical Gloves market is primarily categorized into two types: Natural Latex Surgical Gloves and Synthetic Surgical Gloves. Natural latex gloves are derived from rubber trees, offering excellent elasticity, comfort, and barrier protection; however, they may cause allergic reactions in some users. In contrast, synthetic surgical gloves, made from materials like nitrile or vinyl, provide an alternative free from latex allergens, ensuring safety for sensitive individuals while maintaining a high level of protection and durability during medical procedures.</p></p>
