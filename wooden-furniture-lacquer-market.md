<p><p>The wooden furniture lacquer market includes three main types: Nitrocellulose Lacquers, Acrylic Lacquers, and Water-based Lacquers. Nitrocellulose lacquers offer fast drying times and a durable finish, making them popular for furniture. Acrylic lacquers provide excellent color retention and UV resistance, suitable for high-quality finishes. Water-based lacquers are environmentally friendly, emitting lower VOCs and providing easy cleanup, appealing to eco-conscious consumers. Each type caters to specific needs in terms of application, durability, and environmental impact.</p></p>
