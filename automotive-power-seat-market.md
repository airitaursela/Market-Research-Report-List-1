<p><p>The automotive power seat market can be divided into two primary types: fully automatic and semi-automatic seats. Fully automatic seats offer advanced features, allowing comprehensive adjustments—such as position, lumbar support, and angle—controlled by electronic systems, often with memory settings for multiple users. In contrast, semi-automatic seats provide a mix of manual and power adjustments, allowing users to control certain functions electronically while performing others manually. Both types enhance comfort and convenience, catering to diverse consumer preferences and vehicle models.</p></p>
